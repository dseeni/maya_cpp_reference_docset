Class Description

Texture description.

Provides sufficient information to describe how a block of data can be interpreted as a texture.

Examples:
apiMeshShape/apiMeshGeometryOverride.cpp , cgFx/cgfxShaderNode.cpp , customTextureShader/customTextureShaderOverride.h , dx11Shader/dx11Shader.cpp , hwApiTextureTest/hwApiTextureTest.cpp , hwApiTextureTest/hwRendererHelper.cpp , hwApiTextureTest/hwRendererHelperGL.cpp , hwPhongShader/hwPhongShader.cpp , simpleNoiseShader/simpleNoiseShaderOverride.cpp , and viewImageBlitOverride/viewImageBlitOverride.h .
